# TXTRS-AV Source Links

## Purpose

This note records official source URLs for `txtrs-av` documents that are part
of the intended source set but could not yet be downloaded into the repo by
command-line tools because the TRS site returned anti-bot HTML instead of the
PDF payload.

The source documents are still valid candidates for the `txtrs-av` prep set.
Their official URLs are recorded here and in `source_registry.csv` until local
copies are acquired.

## Official URLs

- 2022 Actuarial Experience Study
  - https://www.trs.texas.gov/TRS%20Documents/actuarial_experience_study_2022.pdf.pdf
- 2024 GASB 67 Reporting and Disclosure Information
  - https://www.trs.texas.gov/files/migrated/gasb-report-67-2024.pdf

## Current Status

- browser-based access works and the content was inspected for this prep pass
- command-line download returned an Incapsula anti-bot HTML page rather than a
  PDF
- local files should be added later if acquired manually or through a workflow
  that can satisfy the site protection

## Why They Matter

- the experience study is important for retiree-mortality methodology and for
  the broader assumption-setting context adopted into later valuations
- the GASB 67 report may help later when calibration-related or liability-
  decomposition questions arise, even though it is not the authoritative source
  document for AV-first prep
